We want to verify our code's performance by replicating the work of [^1] on the
toroidal lattice, as well as extend their results to lattices on the sphere.
This package provides several recipes to automatically generate honeycomb
`TailedLattice`s on either the sphere or the torus.

```@docs
rectangular_honeycomb_grid_on_sphere
rectangular_honeycomb_grid_on_torus
```
